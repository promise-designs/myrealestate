About Wanderlust Adventures

Our Story

Founded in 2008 by travel enthusiasts Sarah and Michael Johnson, Wanderlust Adventures began as a small boutique travel agency specializing in unique, off-the-beaten-path experiences.

What started as a passion project between two college friends has grown into one of the most trusted names in luxury and adventure travel, with offices in New York, London, and Sydney.

Our team of travel experts has personally visited every destination we offer, ensuring we provide authentic, first-hand recommendations tailored to your travel style.

Our Mission

To inspire meaningful connections between people and places through transformative travel experiences that are sustainable, authentic, and unforgettable. We believe travel has the power to open minds, bridge cultures, and create lasting memories.
